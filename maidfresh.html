About Maid Fresh

Kia ora, I’m Jacqueline, the owner of Maid Fresh.

I started this business with a simple goal to provide reliable, high quality cleaning services that people can trust. I understand how important it is to have a clean, comfortable space, whether it’s your home, your business, or a guest ready Airbnb.

With a strong eye for detail and a genuine passion for what I do, I take pride in delivering results that go beyond expectations. From regular home cleans to deep cleans, end of tenancy cleans, and Airbnb turnovers, every job is completed with care, consistency, and professionalism.

I also understand that every client is different, which is why I’m happy to work with you to create a cleaning plan that suits your needs and budget without compromising on quality.

At Maid Fresh, it’s not just about cleaning it’s about creating spaces that feel fresh, welcoming, and ready to enjoy.
